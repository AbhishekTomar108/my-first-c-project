Online Reputation Management Tools

Online reputation is as critical as credibility in the physical world because the reputation online finally transcends into the real world. No doubt, brands, and marketers today spend a great deal of their time and money on managing the online reputation of their businesses and hence they require potent online reputation management services.
 
These are ten tools that you help you track and manage your organization ’s online reputation meticulously:

  • Google Alerts
  • Social Mention
  • Trackur  
  • SentiOne  
  • Reputology
  • TweetBeep
  • BlogPulse
  • Reputation Health
  • Brand24
  • Chatmeter  

Google Alerts

The world ’s favorite search engine, Google has this in-built feature called Google Alerts that when activated notifies the user as soon as activity happens around the activated content. Most brands enter keywords specific to their niche to get alerts. The moment there is a search or an update happening in the online world pertaining to the mentioned keywords, Google sends out an email.

Social Mention

This is a tool that aids marketers to track and monitor when their brands or any other aspect of their industry is being discussed or spoken about on social media platforms. This way they stay on the top of industry-specific social media conversations and deliberations. Brands can get information about the reach, passion, sentiments, and strengths of their business vis-à-vis the competition.

Trackur  

This is another social media tool that is known for its ability to monitor millions of online pages, especially social media sites and platforms to conduct specific keyword-related and trend-related searches. The purpose is to scan all relevant online data available and send alerts to the brand about all and any activity that happens centered on the chosen keywords.

SentiOne  

Here is one of the most commonly used tools for online reputation. The tool is designed to gather pertinent information centering around online customer reviews and feedback about the brand, not only the contemporary ones but also the historical ones. The tool digs out all the relevant information to put the brand in complete control of its present and past market impressions.

Reputology

As the name suggests, this is a tool that is used by marketers to gather as much information as possible from all conceivable online sites and platforms pertaining to the brand. From customer reviews, assessments, and feedback to expert views on the brand –everything is collated and presented in one place helping brands to work on their online reputation effectively.

TweetBeep

This tool is made specially to interpret brand-specific information, updates, and assessments on Twitter. Provided as a free service by the social media giant, this tool helps track every kind of possible movement to and fro, on a given handle. It is an alert generated from the platform helping users to monitor and track every single activity concerning their handle.

BlogPulse

This is a tool that works like a search engine, searching blogs in the online world. Working as a trend analyzer, the platform searches for trends, conversations, profiles, buzzes, top stories, top blogs, top people, and top sources along with top keywords and phrases. This way, bloggers and brands can get to know how their sites and blogs are getting ranked, vis-à-vis competitors.

Reputation Health

This marketing tool is designed specifically for the pharma industry and healthcare providers. A tool that proves to be really helpful for clinics, doctors, and physicians because it assesses and evaluates the top 23 medical review sites digging out reviews, feedback, and ratings from patients about the doctor and his practices. The tool then sends emails and messages to the brand thereafter.

Brand24

Brand24 is an interesting marketing tool that helps in creating projects. Each project tracks an entity ’s social mentions. In some cases, when working with multiple projects, following multiple brands, or different campaigns for one brand, this tool proves to be quite handy. Using specific keywords, the tool makes it easier for things to be identified. The tool not only successfully pulls in mentions from social media but also crawls through the World Wide Web looking if non-excluded keywords are occurring. It collects publicly available mentions in real-time in news sites, socials, blogs, videos, discussion forums, websites, Google reviews, and multiple other sources. It also helps in filtering data and sources to correctly identify the most significant sources. 

Chatmeter  

Brands that are present at multiple locations can use this tool to dig out essential and key information about the competition. The tool looks for local searches and analyses local review sites to compile the information. Brands are then notified via emails. Using this tool, brands can control their online reputation by uploading reviews from other sites onto the brand website.

Using any of these ORM tools can help a company salvage its lost reputation and keep a check on minimizing the dangers of negative and harmful comments. It is always better to be safe and protect the brand image and reputation to avoid challenges in the future.

10 Online Reputation Management Tips For Businesses

ORM services have the power to break or make a business. Business owners have to be very careful when it comes to negative reviews if it shows up and your customers witness them you will be in big trouble. Therefore, managing your online reputation with online reputation management services will help in solving such issues. Given below are some of the online helpful reputation tips.

  1. Keep the focus on the search results
  2. Check the search results regularly

  1. Keep the focus on the search results:

    When it comes to online reputation, an individual 's name or their brand 's name holds a great deal of importance. This will allow them to maintain control over their online reputation and to achieve this they require effective online reputation management services.     It is highly requested that one must search for its company once each month. When an individual 's company can be seen frequently on the news, then the searching must be done frequently as well. Setting up alerts for the keywords that are related to an individual 's brand or business name, will enable them to receive a notification when new content for their businesses is shared online. You can opt for reputation management services for individuals or online reputation management services for companies as reputed ORM services companies provide both.

    There are various ways through which, business owners can take the chance to request or encourage their fellow customers to provide positive reviews. On certain occasions, a simple request or a gentle reminder always does the magic. Individuals can ask their customers about the services they provided and whether or not it exceeded their expectations.   7. Create and start a blog:

How Much Do ORM Services Cost?

When it comes to online reputation management (ORM) services, the cost is one of the most important factors for businesses. ORM services can be expensive or affordable depending on the complexity of your project and the type of service you require. Generally speaking, however, ORM services are not overly expensive and offer great value for money.

For basic needs such as monitoring reviews, responding to customer feedback, and optimizing social media profiles, many companies charge a flat fee per month or a low hourly rate. The cost of ORM services also depends on how much effort is required to achieve your desired results as well as any additional features that may be necessary.
