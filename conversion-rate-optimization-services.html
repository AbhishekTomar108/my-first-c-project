Heatmap and click tracking are two of the most important tools used in conversion rate optimization. Heatmaps help to visualize user behavior on a website by displaying how visitors interact with different elements, while click tracking allows you to track clicks on certain elements of your website. By combining these two powerful tools, businesses can gain valuable insights into their customer 's journeys and optimize their website for better conversions. Heatmaps provide an easy way to understand how users interact with different aspects of a website. They display user activity in visual form, helping webmasters recognize patterns and make informed decisions about changes that need to be made for higher conversions. For example, if the heatmap shows that many users hover over an element but few actually click it, then the element could be redesigned or adjusted so more people will take action when they see it. Heatmaps also allows businesses to identify which areas of the page are receiving the most attention from visitors and make adjustments accordingly.

When you work with a leading conversion rate optimization company like ours, we can help you set up and analyze heat maps to better understand how visitors interact with your website.
